St Joseph Farmstead

The over 100 year old farm produces vegetables, fruit, and beef in an environmentally sustainable way. Food produced on the farm is sold in the Farm Stand and donated to local pantries. The 60,000 sqft building was built in 1910 and is a great place for events or to rent a studio.
